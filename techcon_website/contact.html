Additional Information

Conference Hours

  • Day 1: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Day 2: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Day 3: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Registration Desk

The registration desk will be open 30 minutes before the conference starts each day and will remain open throughout the event for any assistance you may need.

Parking Information

Complimentary parking is available at the convention center. Additional paid parking options are available within a 2-block radius of the venue.

Public Transportation

The venue is easily accessible by public transportation:

  • Metro: Montgomery Street Station (2 blocks away)
  • Bus Lines: 1, 8, 12, 30, 45
  • BART: Embarcadero Station (3 blocks away)

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the conference?

Please bring your registration confirmation (digital or printed), a valid photo ID, business cards for networking, and any mobile devices you'd like to use for taking notes or connecting with other attendees.

Is there a dress code?

Business casual attire is recommended. The venue is climate-controlled, so dress comfortably for indoor activities.

Will meals be provided?

Yes! Breakfast, lunch, and coffee breaks are included with your registration. We accommodate various dietary restrictions - please let us know when you register.

Can I get a refund if I cannot attend?

Refund policies vary by registration type. Please refer to your registration confirmation email or contact us directly for specific information about your registration.
